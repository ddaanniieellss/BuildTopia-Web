Welcome to BuildTopia

BuildTopia is a mmo game where you can gather resources and make your own worlds! You can fight enemies, complete quests, craft items, make friends and much more.

Build anything

Almost everything in this game is player created. You have the tools to create anything you can imagine, and instantly share it with other players. Create parkour, pixel art, houses, stories, or your own minigames.
